Data Deletion Request

Right Touch Partner App

We respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data from the Right Touch Partner App, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

Technicians may request account deletion by sending an email to our support team.

Email: support@righttouch.in

Subject: Data Deletion Request - [Your Phone Number/ID]

Send Deletion Request Email

What Happens Next?

  • Processing Time: Please allow 7-15 working days for us to process your request.
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before proceeding with the deletion.

After Deletion

  • Account Access: Your login credentials will be permanently disabled.
  • Profile Status: Your profile will be marked inactive and removed from our search results.
  • Personal Data: Your personal information (name, photo, etc.) will be deleted from our active databases.
  • Retention: Please note that certain data, such as transaction history and financial records, may be retained for a mandatory period as required by applicable tax and legal regulations.

Need Help?

If you have questions about the deletion process, please contact support.

Email: support@righttouch.in
